How it Works

This tool uses Combinatorics to find the minimum number of items needed to create a unique outfit every day.

The Logic

  • Optimized (Winner): We calculate the N-th root of your days (e.g., Square root for 2 categories) to find the perfect balance. We assume you can go over the target days to save money on clothes.
  • Exact (Rigid): We use Prime Factorization to hit the exact number. This often results in weird numbers (like buying 61 shirts for a Leap Year) and costs more.

Why use this?

  • Save Money: Buy fewer clothes, get more outfits.
  • The "Cicada" Effect: By using different pile sizes (like 7 shirts and 3 pants), you avoid repeating patterns, just like Cicadas use prime numbers to avoid predators.
  • Capsule Wardrobe: Perfect for planning a minimalist travel bag or seasonal closet.
